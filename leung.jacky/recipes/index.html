Recipe 1 - Breakfast Omelet

Ingredients

  • - 2 eggs
  • - 1 ham
  • - half green bell pepper
  • - 1/4 onion
  • - 1/2 cup of heavy cream

Cooking Instruction

  • - dice up the ham, bell pepper, and onion
  • - beat the eggs with 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • - heat up the non-stick pan with 1tbsp of cooking oil on medium fire
  • - cook the ham, onion, and bell pepper to 60%
  • - empty the pan, add 1 tbsp of oil
  • - put in the egg and add the rest of ingredients
  • - cook and serve

Enjoy!

Recipe 2 - Seared Octopus

Ingredients

  • - 1 pack of precooked octopus
  • - 1 tea spoon of sea salt
  • - 1 tbsp of olive oil

Cooking Instruction

  • - dice up octopus to small pieces
  • - heat up the pan with olive oil with medium fire
  • - add in the octopus, cook for 3-4 minutes
  • - sprinkle sea salt before serving

Enjoy!

Recipe 3 - Stir Fry Celery with Garlic

Ingredients

  • - 1 bunch of celery
  • - 2 shallots of garlic
  • - 1 teaspoon of sea salt

Cooking Instruction

  • - dice up the celery and garlic
  • - heat up the pan with oil with medium fire
  • - add in the garlic and stir for 1-2 minutes
  • - add in the celery and stir for 4-5 minutes

Enjoy!
